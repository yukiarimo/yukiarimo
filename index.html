About

I'm a full-stack developer who creates software using JavaScript and Python. My work goes beyond just coding. I want to explore the relationship between technology and humanity. As a developer, I build applications encouraging contemplation about life's deeper questions.

Join me on a philosophical inquiry into society, purpose, and love. Together, we'll explore the intricacies of human nature and the ethical challenges in our digital future. Let's navigate the complex web of our interconnected world and seek to understand it.
